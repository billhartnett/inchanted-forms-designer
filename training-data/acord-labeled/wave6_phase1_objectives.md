# wave6_phase1_objectives

## Mission
Launch Wave 6 replay-driven tuning with deterministic replay controls, strict classification telemetry, and gated progression criteria.

## Objectives
1. Establish replay-driven tuning orchestration for mapping evolution with reproducible fixture slices.
2. Integrate strict-mode telemetry carry-forward for host and threshold failure classes.
3. Define replay confidence envelopes and regression deltas as first-class gate metrics.
4. Preserve Wave 4.8 baseline freeze and keep Wave 4 tuning closed while advancing Wave 6 only.

## Success Criteria
- Replay runs are reproducible with fixed seeds and documented fixture sets.
- Failure classifications are emitted for all strict replay gates.
- Wave 6 Phase 1 gates pass without violating Wave 4.8 or Wave 4 guardrails.
